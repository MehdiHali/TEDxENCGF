What is

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TED created a program called TEDx. TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. Our event is called TEDxENCGF, where x = independently organized TED event. The event is a gift to the community, made possible by generous sponsors and volunteers along with hundreds of individuals dedicated to making the community a better place.

TEDxENCGF
TEDxENCGF Beyond is taking place on March 4th, 2022. This event will feature speakers and performers that will take the stage to share their ideas. TEDxEncgf focuses on engaging attendees in a world-class experience by showcasing the best of our community. The uniquely ENCGF experience is created through high-quality event design, local activities and engaging talk topics to spark innovation.
